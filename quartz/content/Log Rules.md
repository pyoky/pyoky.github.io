1. Product Rule:
	- $\log_b{(xy)} = \log_b{x} + \log_b{y}$
2. Quotient Rule:
	- $\log_b{(x/y)} = \log_b{x} - \log_b{y}$
3. Power Rule:
	- $\log_b{x^n} = n \cdot \log_b{x}$
4. Change of Base Rule:
	- $\log_b{a} = \frac{\log_c{a}}{\log_c{b}}$
5. Identity Rule:
	- $\log_b{b} = 1$ and $\log_b{1} = 0$
