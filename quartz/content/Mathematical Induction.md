## Weak Induction \[=Normal Induction]

1. Base case

$$
P(1)\text{ is true }
$$

2. Inductive Hypothesis (IH)

$$
\text{Assume } P(k) \text{ is true}
$$

3. Inductive Step

$$
P(k) \text{ is true } \Rightarrow
P(k+1) \text{ is true}
$$
## Strong Induction

1. Base cases
$$

P(1)\dots P(n)\text{ is true}

$$
2. Inductive Hypothesis (IH)
$$

\text{Assume } P(k) \text{ is true}

$$
3. Inductive Step
$$

P(k) \text{ is true } \Rightarrow
P(k+1) \text{ is true}
$$
