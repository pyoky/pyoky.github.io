Past or ongoing projects. Some projects were formalized and submitted as research for class.

  • Cryptoanalysis of the Enigma Machine

    A research paper on the encryption mechanims of the German Enigma machine, used to encrypt millitary communications during World War Two, and the efforts of Alan Turing and the members of Bletchely Park in its cryptoanalysis.

  • Implementation of Common Axiom Systems and Proof Generation

    Utilizing the function FindEquationalProof introduced in version 11.3 of Mathematica, I implemented and proved common algebraic and logical conjectures as well as hypothetical, but plausible combinations of axioms and conjectures, as introduced in Chapter 12, Section 9 of Stephen Wolfram's book, A New Kind of Science.
